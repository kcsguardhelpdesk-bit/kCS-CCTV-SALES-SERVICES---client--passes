Frequently Asked Questions

Expert answers to your security and infrastructure queries.

How long does a standard CCTV installation take?

A standard 4-camera home installation typically takes 4-6 hours, including wiring and app configuration.

Do your cameras work during power outages?

Yes, when integrated with our UPS solutions, systems remain 100% operational during cuts.

Do you provide after-sales service and maintenance?

Absolutely! We offer complete doorstep repair, system maintenance, and a dedicated 1-year service warranty on all our installations.

Yes, all our CCTV and Smart Camera systems come with an official mobile app that lets you monitor your premises live from anywhere in the world.
